# Sky Guardian

Sky Guardian is an exhilarating arcade 2D game where players become the guardians of the sky. Your mission is to shoot down enemy attacks, including guided missiles, ballistic missiles, kamikaze drones, and bombers. Endless aerial battles await you, where player agility and reaction time are the keys to success. Soar into action, defend the skies, and rack up as many points as possible!

Enemy objects:

## Horizontal Flying Missiles:

These cunning projectiles swiftly approach until they alter their trajectory, attacking your vehicle from the side. Their trickery lies in unexpected maneuvers, requiring swift reactions and precise shooting to destroy them before they get close to your vehicle.

## Ballistic Missiles:

Designed to fall from height, these missiles threaten your vehicle from top to bottom. Their appearance is not always predictable, so you'll need to stay vigilant and keep an eye on the sky to neutralize the threat in time.

## Kamikaze Drones:

These machines attack boldly and with high speed, aiming to collide with your automobile. Their goal is to destroy you in close combat, so be prepared for intense encounters and accurate shooting to thwart their assault.

## Bomber Aircraft:

Aircraft of this type pose a threat from the air, dropping vertically falling bombs. You'll need to quickly recognize their approach and coordinate your shooting to destroy the bombs before they cause damage to your vehicle and the surrounding world.
